Bookstore Info & Policies

About Our Bookstore
We are an online bookstore specializing in a curated selection of fiction, non-fiction, programming, science, self-help, and business books. We operate entirely through WhatsApp for a convenient and personal shopping experience.

Store Hours
Our WhatsApp support is available Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM (Sri Lanka time, GMT+5:30). The AI assistant is available 24/7 for browsing books and placing orders. Admin order confirmations and receipt verifications are handled during business hours.

How to Order
1. Send us a message on WhatsApp to browse our catalog.
2. Tell us which books you'd like and we'll add them to your cart.
3. When ready, confirm your order and we'll provide bank transfer details.
4. Transfer the payment and send us a photo of the receipt.
5. Once verified, we'll ship your order.

Payment Methods
We accept bank transfers only. After confirming your order, you'll receive our bank account details. Please transfer the exact order amount and send a clear photo of the transfer receipt via WhatsApp. Payments are typically verified within 1-2 business hours during store hours.

Shipping & Delivery
We offer island-wide delivery within Sri Lanka. Orders are shipped within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation. Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days. Colombo and suburbs may receive orders within 1-2 days. Shipping is free for orders above Rs. 5,000. A flat shipping fee of Rs. 350 applies for orders below Rs. 5,000.

Return & Refund Policy
Books may be returned within 7 days of delivery if they arrive damaged or if the wrong book was sent. To initiate a return, send us a message with your order number and a photo of the issue. Refunds are processed within 5 business days via bank transfer to your original payment account. We do not accept returns for change of mind.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I order multiple books at once?
A: Yes! You can add as many books as you want to your cart before confirming the order.

Q: Do you offer gift wrapping?
A: Not at this time, but we plan to add this service soon.

Q: Can I cancel my order?
A: Yes, you can cancel before payment verification. Once payment is confirmed and the order is being shipped, cancellation is not possible.

Q: Do you have e-books?
A: Currently we only sell physical books. E-book support may be added in the future.

Q: Can I track my order?
A: Yes, just ask us about your order status anytime and we'll give you an update.
